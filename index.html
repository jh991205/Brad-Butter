Fall Japanese Curry

silent screams through

white noise streams

her dreams don’t seem to fade

the drying petals

caress her lonely face

he just can't come to;

can't see himself through;

not like the others do.

drowns out the entourage and starts his life anew

Lemon Miso Noodle Soup with Tempura Lemon

dreams end on

fogged stained glasses.

can’t face the masses

without the rocks piled

glasses on glasses on glasses

Peanut Butter & Apple Layer Cake

it’s hard to forget,

screams of a sun that bathe in its own addiction

the supple rays who

coax the blind, oblivious to that

bottomless void that resides within.

pure bliss in

comfort, less the devil;

says the mind.

and we all know

better, but

where else can we go.

White Chocolate Lavender Lava Cake

dreams of firelit faces

and undefined cases.

he flirts with perfection,

incapable of accepting his grievances.

these crooked smiles and

tilted tiles. they

yearn for the warmth

of the damp winter night.

so be it,

beauty is seldom intentional
