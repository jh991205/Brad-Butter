White Chocolate Lavender Lava Cake

"That moist, delicate cake stuffed with a hot, creamy filling that just oozes out — pastry perfection."

dreams of firelit faces

and undefined cases.

he flirts with perfection,

incapable of accepting his grievances.

Peanut Butter and Apple Layer Cake

"There was something about the cold, crisp apples paired and the smooth nutty spread that I couldn’t get over."

it’s hard to forget,

screams of a sun that bathe in its own addiction

the supple rays who

coax the blind, oblivious to that

bottomless void that resides within.

pure bliss in

comfort, less the devil;

says the mind.

and we all know

better, but

where else can we go.
