Food, The Glue That Held My Family Together

chains rattle,

locks click,

cubes clink,

sheets shift

a mere glimpse to

engineer the scope out of

perceived smiles and laughs.

yet

chains rattle,

locks click,

cubes clink,

sheets shift

beneath the facade

of the harth,

the liquid burn crawls in to

the mind, relentless in

remembrance.

and still

chains rattle,

locks click,

cubes clink,

sheets shift

one day

chains will rattle,

locks will click,

cubes will clink,

but will those green sheets shift.

My Takeaway From The Culinary Experience

return trips for

frozen treats and off-burnt ends

we eat pretty.

beguiled foolishness

finds bliss

we drink pretty.

music is letter and space

stitching hearts and colors

we sing pretty.

joy, a memento of the air

our breaths draw

we live pretty.

beneath every tear,

scar, and lie; you are pretty.
